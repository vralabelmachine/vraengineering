About VRA Engineering Works

Excellence in Garment Label Machinery & Manufacturing since 2011

Our Story

Established in **2011**, VRA Engineering Works has grown to become a cornerstone in the garment label industry. With over **14+ years of experience**, we have dedicated ourselves to supplying high-performance machinery and precision-engineered custom parts.

We take pride in being an **Authorised Distributor of Vertex**, a partnership that allows us to bring world-class label cutting and folding technology to our clients. Our commitment extends beyond distribution; we understand the unique needs of our customers, which led us to establish our own manufacturing unit.

Today, VRA Engineering Works is synonymous with trust, accuracy, and technical expertise. We not only supply machinery but also manufacture **21+ specialized machine parts** designed to keep your operations running smoothly.
